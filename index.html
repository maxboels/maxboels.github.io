    Predicting Breast Cancer

    Deep Learning approach

    Radiologists are cautious during screenings, the consequences of having a false negative push them to send women for biopsy if there is the slightest doubt for them to have cancer.
    A deep learning approach is used to classify abnormal screenings as either malignant or benign cancer with a certain probability.
    Transfer Learning makes it possible to obtain high performances on small datasets.
    This project achieved a ROC of 80%, 86% sensitivity, and 77% NPV, which were reached with a pre-trained ResNet50v2, a state-of-the-art neural network optimized through fine-tuning hyperparameters and data pre-processing.
